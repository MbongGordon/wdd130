Wild Rapids Rafting Company

Experience the thrill of white water rafting with our expert guides and top-quality equipment. We are committed to providing safe, exciting, and memorable adventures for all skill levels. Our mission is to connect people with the power and beauty of nature while creating lasting memories on the rapids. Join us for an unforgettable journey down the most spectacular rivers!

History

Founded in 1985 by adventure enthusiasts Mike and Sarah Thompson, Wild Rapids Rafting Company began as a small family business with just two rafts and a passion for the outdoors. Over the decades, we have grown into the region's premier rafting outfitter, serving thousands of adventurers annually while maintaining our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and exceptional customer service. Our experienced guides have navigated these waters for generations, sharing their knowledge of local wildlife, geology, and river conservation with every guest who joins our expeditions.
