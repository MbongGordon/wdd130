Our Rafting Adventures

Choose your perfect adventure from our carefully crafted trips designed for every skill level and preference.

Family Float Adventure

Difficulty: Easy | Duration: 3 Hours

Perfect for families with children and first-time rafters. This gentle journey down the Crystal River features Class I-II rapids with plenty of calm water for swimming and wildlife watching. Our guides will point out eagles, otters, and other local wildlife while sharing stories about the region's natural history. Includes a riverside picnic lunch and all necessary safety equipment. Ages 5 and up welcome.

  • Gentle Class I-II rapids
  • Wildlife viewing opportunities
  • Riverside picnic included
  • Perfect for ages 5+

Thunder Canyon Expedition

Difficulty: Moderate | Duration: 5 Hours

Experience the perfect balance of excitement and scenery on our most popular trip. Thunder Canyon offers thrilling Class III rapids interspersed with calmer sections through stunning granite gorges. You'll tackle rapids with names like "The Washing Machine" and "Pinball" while enjoying breathtaking mountain views. This full-day adventure includes a gourmet lunch on a sandy beach and time for cliff jumping in a deep pool. Ages 12 and up, swimming ability required.

  • Exciting Class III rapids
  • Gourmet riverside lunch
  • Optional cliff jumping
  • Stunning canyon scenery

Devil's Gorge Challenge

Difficulty: Extreme | Duration: 7 Hours
